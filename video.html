The video was created through compiling clips from 12 different videos. Drone and aerial footage was valued above ground work, not just for aesthetics but also to give the viewer a sense of the bigger picture. A sharp transition in music midway shocks the audience, further adding to the atmosphere of dread in the second half of the video.

The main issues focused on are deforestation, mining, coal factories and global warming. These issues are intricately connected and the main drivers of enviromental destruction. As many as 55,600 trees are cut down a minute. This is destruction on an untold scale. We need to take action, and we need to take it now.
