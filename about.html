nature.

about.

Our Earth is a precious resource in itself - a safe haven for millions upon millions of unique species, each with hundreds of millions of years of heritage. It's unlike any other planet we know of; and it is also the very thing keeping us alive. Yet we treat it like a discardable consumable - draining it of resources recklessly, with little regard for the future. nature is a short film that hopes to capture the beauty of Earth, and make people aware of the damage we are doing to it.

The social issue being addressed here is the most important of all; the way we treat our species home. It is my hope that by showing how beautiful this planet is, and then showing how we are hurting it (logging, burning, coal factories, mining), I can elict a strong emotional response.
